Djing and Producing Techno

Reasons why I like Techno

The country where I was born, Georgia, has a huge fan base for techno music.
I enjoy the deep rhythms and the way techno creates an atmosphere where people can lose themselves in sound.
It’s also inspiring to see how DJs and producers mix tracks live to keep the crowd moving.
For me, techno is not just music, it’s a culture and a form of self-expression.
